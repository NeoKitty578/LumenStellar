History

Lumen-Stellar was founded in early 2026 by a small group of astronomers who wanted to create a digital platform where people could learn and explore more about the universe and its vastness. The platform was designed to make astronomy more accessible and engaging for everyone, especially students and curious minds interested in galaxies, stars, black holes, planets, and the possibility of life beyond Earth. Through interactive articles, space news, educational content, and stunning visuals, Lumen-Stellar aims to inspire a deeper curiosity about the cosmos and encourage future generations to continue exploring the mysteries of the universe.

About us:

Vision:

The vision of Lumen-Stellar is to inspire the next generation of astronomers, astrophysicists, space scientists, cosmologists and innovators to pursue more knowledge about space.

Mission:

The mission of Lumen-Stellar is to make space science accessible for everyone by providing educational and interactive content about the universe.
